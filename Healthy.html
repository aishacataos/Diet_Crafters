Breakfast

Start your day with a burst of sweetness and essential nutrients. Your breakfast includes ripe mango, rich in vitamins C and A, fiber, and antioxidants. Accompanying this is a poached egg, offering high-quality protein and omega-3 fatty acids, served alongside whole wheat bread for your daily dose of fiber and complex carbohydrates. A glass of fresh milk provides calcium for bone health and completes this balanced morning meal.

AM Snack

Indulge in a delightful mid-morning treat with our "Turon", a healthier version of the traditional Filipino snack. Made with banana and a hint of jackfruit wrapped in a thin pastry and lightly pan-fried in minimal oil, it offers a satisfying crunch with a sweet and savory filling, providing an energy boost with controlled sugar levels.

Lunch

Savor the flavors of stir-fried pork combined withchicharo (peas) and green beans, cooked in a light, savory sauce. This dish is packed with lean protein, vitamins, and antioxidants, promoting muscle health and digestion. Paired with boiled rice for a comforting, fulfilling base, and complemented by chilled ripe langka (jackfruit), adding a sweet, fiber-rich end to your meal.

Dinner

Wind down with a comforting bowl of "Ginulay na Mais at Malunggay", a corn and moringa leaf soup. This dish provides a high fiber content, essential vitamins, and minerals, supporting immune health and digestion. Accompanied by Chicken Lumpia, a lean meat spring roll that's baked instead of fried, and a side of boiled rice, it's a satisfying end to the day. A fresh slice of watermelon, packed with hydration and antioxidants, offers a refreshing, sweet conclusion to your meals.
