Dinner

Ginulay na mais at malunggay, Chicken Lumpia, White Rice and Watermelon

Ingredients

Ginulay na mais at Malunggay

  • 1 cup fresh corn kernels (or 1 cup frozen corn, thawed)
  • 1 cup chopped malunggay leaves
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1-2 cups water (or vegetable broth)

Chicken Lumpia

Ingredients:

For the filling:

  • 1/2 pound ground chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/4 cup chopped celery
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup chopped green beans (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil (for sauteing)

For the wrapper:

  • 1 pack lumpia wrappers (around 20-25 pieces)
  • 1 cup water

Nutrient:

Nutrient Amount
Calories 120 kcal
Protein 4 g

White Rice

Nutritional information for a typical serving of white rice (around 1 cup cooked, or 185 grams):

Nutrient Amount
Calories 205 kcal

Carbohydrates 45 g

Meal Procedure

  1. In a pot or pan, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the corn kernels and cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the water (or vegetable broth) and bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until the corn is tender.
  6. Add the chopped malunggay leaves and cook for another minute, until slightly wilted.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
