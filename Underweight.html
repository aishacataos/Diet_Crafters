AM Snack

Recharge with a mid-morning snack designed to boost your energy levels. This snack is "Buchi", a popular snack in Filipino cuisine, known for its sweet and savory flavors. It's a type of rice cake, made from glutinous rice flour, and traditionally filled with sweetened red bean paste, though variations might include fillings like ube, custard, or even chocolate for a variety of healthy fat and nutrients.

Lunch

Enjoy a hearty lunch that combines complex carbohydrates, lean proteins, and healthy fats to support your weight gain goals. Today's meal features chicken pochero with veggies, boiled rice, and kamachile.

PM Snack

Don't be afraid to spoil yourself with some Pancit Canton. A classic favorite that comes in an assortment of flavor and best taken in moderation (not daily). You can also mix this up with more nutritious food like boiled egg/s and vegetables (scallions, carrots, or lettuce)!

Dinner

End your day with a dinner that not only satisfies your appetite but also contributes to your nutritional needs. Your dinner includes a generous serving of sinigang na talakitok with veggies, pork strip fritters, and mango-sago with gelatin ensuring a well-rounded intake of vitamins and minerals
